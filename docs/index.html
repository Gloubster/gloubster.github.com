Introduction

Why you should not process anything on your production webserver

As job processing is potentially CPU intensive, it is the main gate for a denial of service attack. You should prevent any commandline (build of zip files for example) or big resource consumers at runtime (Image resize, video compression).

These tasks should be done asynchronously and limited so that the load could not be impacted by a traffic increase.

Distribute your jobs

Gloubster distributes your jobs among workers so it's very scalable.

Receive your results where you want

Gloubster can deliver data nearly anywhere. Current implementations now deliver to Amazon S3 and filesystem, but you can of course implement your own in few lines.

Monitor you workers

Gloubster provides a complete monitoring solution, you can build your own deployment based on it.
